Solar General

Solar is considered to be the best investment as it allows you to save money on monthly electricity bills and also helps in maintaining environmental sustainability by reducing annual carbon emission in the atmosphere.
The solar systems which are installed on the (empty) roof or terrace of your house or building are referred to as a rooftop solar system.
For installing a solar system you need to check the following factors:
● Your electricity consumption
● Min 100 sq ft roof space
● Shadow free roof
Net metering is a billing mechanism that credits house owners for the electricity they add to the utility grid when their solar system generates electricity in excess.
The solar system works on cloudy days but on low efficiency with nearly 40% of the optimal power generation.

Solar Economics

The cost of the solar system depends upon the size, configuration, and degree of supporting structures required. We provide you multiple options to choose from designed specifically to meet your requirements.
Rooftop Solar projects are the most lucrative investments for industrial, residential, and commercial sectors as the break-even is achieved by 2-4 years, while you enjoy the savings for 25 years.
For more details, you can visit the MNRE website.
There are no subsidies available for commercial entities. For residential rooftop projects the state government offers subsidies intermittently.

Solar Maintenance

The lifespan of an accurately installed solar rooftop system is about 25 years. Where the main components are solar - panels and inverter. Solar panels come with a production warranty of 25years while solar inverters have a warranty of 5-12 years.
Rooftop Solar system does not involve any moving parts hence the system requires very little maintenance. It is recommended to clean the panels 1-2 times a month.
At NxtGen we ensure that no damage takes place to existing structures during installation and running of solar plants. We use a multitude of techniques like non-penetrating structures and waterproofing solutions to ensure your roof's integrity.
